WHO WE ARE

Myanmar's Specialist Precision Instrument Service Center

Myanmar Precision Systems is Yangon's dedicated service center for high-precision measurement instruments. Our certified technicians handle everything from field survey equipment to geotechnical testing apparatus — providing expert repair, ISO-traceable calibration, and preventive maintenance that keeps your instruments accurate and your projects on schedule.

Products are available on request — contact us for sourcing, rental arrangements, or equipment recommendations.

OUR SERVICES

Complete Instrument Service Solutions

From field damage to workshop overhaul — we restore accuracy.

Repair & Overhaul

Full disassembly, cleaning, component replacement, and reassembly. Covers mechanical, electronic, and optical faults.

Calibration & Certification

ISO-traceable calibration with official certificate. Accepted for project compliance and tender requirements.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled servicing to detect wear, clean optics, and firmware check before failures occur in the field.

On-site Technical Support

Certified technicians dispatched to your project site for calibration and emergency repairs across Myanmar.
